## 2026-02-12 - Broken 'Clear' Button Frustrates Users
**Learning:** A non-functional 'Clear' button is worse than no 'Clear' button. It sets an expectation of a quick reset but delivers a dead end, forcing manual deletion of each field.
**Action:** Always verify that buttons implying a 'reset' or 'clear' action are functionally bound to a method that resets the UI state completely.
